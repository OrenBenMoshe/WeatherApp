Welcome to my weather forecast

As a frequent traveler, I am always interested in staying up to date on the weather forecast for my destination. This is especially important when it comes to planning my outfits and packing for my trip. I want to be prepared for any unexpected weather changes and make sure that I have the right clothes and gear with me.

In this App you can get the weather for the next few days.

  • Enter a desirable city.
  • Select the time segment.
  • Choose between Fahrenheit/Celsius
  • Click the button and get the data
